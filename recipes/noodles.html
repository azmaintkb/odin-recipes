Noodles Recipe

Description

Yo Bro ! You know it right? haha ! Obviously you know cause it's a bachelor's life saving food :))

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp JS Garlic Pepper
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 50 ml water
  • 3 carrots
  • 1 leek
  • 3 spring onions
  • 4 tbsp sunflower oil

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles in plenty of water according to the packet instructions, drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. Break the eggs into a bowl and mix with 1/2 tsp Garlic Pepper seasoning. Mix the other 1/2 tsp Garlic Pepper with the soy sauce and water in a small bowl.
  3. Peel the carrots, cut into thin slices and then into very thin strips. Halve the leek, wash and slice into thin half-moons. Wash and clean the spring onions and slice thinly.
  4. Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a wok pan, add the egg mixture and cook for a minute, stirring constantly. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  5. Pour the remaining oil into the pan and add the noodles. Stir-fry for two minutes. Add the carrots and leek, fry for 3 minutes, deglaze with the soy sauce and stir well.
  6. Add the eggs and spring onions and mix well.
